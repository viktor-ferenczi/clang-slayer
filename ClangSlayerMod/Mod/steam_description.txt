[h1]Clang Slayer[/h1]

Suppresses clang in Space Engineers.

[h2]How to use[/h2]
[list]
[*] Add this mod to your world.
[*] Try to use a clang drive or weapon. They should not work.
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
[list]
[*] Works in both single and multiplayer.
[*] Works in both creative and survival game modes.
[/list]

[h2]Theory of operation[/h2]
The mod 
[list]
[*] tracks all mechanical connection bases
[*] compares the intended head position and orientation with the actual one
[*] measures the strain (clang forces) from the difference in position and orientation
[*] temporarily changes the parameters of the mechanical connection bases to "tune down" clang
[/list]
